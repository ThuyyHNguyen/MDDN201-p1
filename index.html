Wellington Infrastructure

Security and Surviellance

There are many type of Security and Surviellance around Wellington and especially Wellington CBD. Many of them are privte securities and some of them are street securities. Surviellance and security can be anytype such as cameras, keycard swipe and fingerprint. These are some of the examples of the security and surviellance commonly seen around Wellington.

1.Private Building Entrance Key Card Swipe

Mostly every residence building or private building have their own swipe card access. The access is restricted to everyone and it heavily depends on electricity to operate. The card swipe is unique to the building and an only number of keys card can access this building to open the door for entry. This particular one is set at one of the flat/apartment type building in Wellington and only residence or landlord can access the building.

2. Private Car Park Key Card Swipe

Similar to private building and residence building, there is some cars park also equipped with the Key Card Swipe to control the automatic garage door. The entry is only for authorized people and it is not open for public uses.

3. Street Security Camera

Around Wellington, there are many cameras around the city for security or surveillance reason. This is one of the cameras in the CBD area. It appears mostly on the wall in the alley area because it can be more dangerous in the alley than on the street.

4. Private apartment/flat/residence camera

Every apartment or flat in Wellington is equipped with many security cameras for safety reasons. Firstly, it protects residents from trespassing or unauthorized access the building. Secondly, it could prevent many robberies happen to the residents in the building. Also, it can prevent some unwanted damage inside or outside the building.

5. Apartment Bell

The bell can give the visitor access to the building. The visitor can ring the apartment number bell and the person at the apartment can open the door when still at the apartment. The residence can decide to open the door for the visitor when they pick up the phone from their apartment and speak to the visitor.

Traffic and Transport

There are many ways to travel around Wellington either by public transportation or by private transportation. The public transportation such as bus and train always have timetable at their own station or online on the website. When you travel around the city using the private transportation, you need to park your vehicle at the parking lot which available in the city center.

1.Bus stop timetable

The bus stop timetables are available around Wellington and each station has their own time display and the timetable also has online access. Everyone can use the payment for the bus which is link to the internet to automatically charge the ride.

2. Parking lot sign

When people go to the city by their private transportation, they need a place to park their vehicles. There are many parking lot around Wellington but they are not availabled everytime. The solution is having a camera at the parking lot to see if there is any available space then it will show on the sign. It also show the price for parking aswell.

3. Parking Payment Machine

The parking payment machine is everywhere around the city and it available at the street parking and in the parking lot. The payment is made via Snapper or card payment. The machine is connected to internet so it can accept or decline the payment.

4. Traffic Light

Traffic Lights around the city are followed a system which make the traffic more smoother. Every traffic lights are connected to each other so it can minimise the traffic jam and also to control the vehicles come from many direction.

5. Traffic Light Button

This button is control the traffic light for the walking people on the street. This light turn on the signal for the walking people so everyone know when to go in order to keep them safe. This button also available on every traffic light and it connect to each other so the system know when to stop the vehicles to give ways to everyone.

Public Infrastructure

There are many interaction around Wellington involve in Internet. The internet infrastructure can be easily found on the street or public building.

1.Spark Free Wifi

The Free Wifi booth is available for Spark user only. It is not 100% public wifi but it is available everytime. There are many free wifi area around the city which can be easily found.

2. Spark on 36 Willis st

This is the place to provide internet for everyone such as 3G, 4G and home broadband. This is the small part of the big coporate to provide connection to everyone.

3. Vending Machine

This is a vending machine at Victoria University of Wellingtion. This is an example of public infrastructure and this type of machine is available around the city. The machine is used to be limited around coin and cash payment but at the moment it is upgrated to connect to internet to make card payment available.
